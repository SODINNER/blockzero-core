# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and constructive community.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and technically honest.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Help newcomers participate.
- Avoid hype, harassment, or manipulation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insults
- Discriminatory language
- Spam, scams, or deceptive promotion
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior violates these standards.
